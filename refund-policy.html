Refund & Exchange Policy
We want you to feel confident with every purchase. Our refund and exchange policy is designed to be fair, transparent, and focused on your satisfaction.
Eligibility for Refunds & Exchanges
If you receive an item that does not meet your expectations or arrives in unsatisfactory condition, you may request a refund or exchange within 7 days of receiving your order.
  • Items must be unused, unwashed, and in their original packaging.
  • Proof of purchase is required for all refund or exchange requests.
  • Customized or clearance items may not be eligible unless defective.
How to Initiate a Return
To start your refund or exchange process, please contact our support team by email or phone. Provide your order details, a description of the issue, and photos if applicable. We will guide you through the next steps.
Once your request is approved, you'll receive instructions for returning your item. Refunds are typically processed within 5 business days of receiving your returned item.
Refund Methods
Refunds will be issued via your original payment method or as store credit, based on your preference. Exchanges can be made for items of equal or lesser value.
Non-Refundable Items
For hygiene reasons, accessories and undergarments may not be eligible for return unless they are defective. Clearance or customized products are not refundable unless there is a manufacturing issue.
Returns received after 7 days from delivery or items not meeting eligibility conditions will not be accepted.
Shipping for Returns
Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or the wrong item was delivered. Please use a trackable shipping service, and keep your receipt until your refund or exchange is complete.
Policy Updates
This refund policy may be updated as needed. We encourage you to review this page for the most current information before making a purchase.
